Jeremy Clarkson's head now available for Hungry Hungry Hippos

All you need is a 3D printer to ensure Jezza never misses another meal. Fracas: averted!
By Alex Nunez
Mar 23, 2015
CEL Robox

The Top Gear fracas that left Jeremy Clarkson's tenure at the BBC in doubt allegedly started over a meal not being ready. Now, thanks to the team behind the CEL Robox 3D printer, Jezza needn't ever miss another meal.

That's because the company has posted a new printable object—Jeremy Clarkson's head—that can be retrofitted onto the Hungry Hungry Hippos game sitting in your basement:
